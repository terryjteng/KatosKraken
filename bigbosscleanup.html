Welcome to Big Boss Cleanup

After the final boss falls and the street fights finish, someone still has to mop up the mess. That someone... is you. Grab your mop, dodge the debris, and clean up the carnage left behind by heroes too busy basking in glory.

Gameplay

The game is an arcade-style cleanup challenge where each level represents a new battlefield with unique hazards and environmental danger. Dodge lingering projectiles, maneuver through debris, and rack up style points for efficient and stylish cleaning.

Still in Early Development

Big Boss Cleanup is currently in the early concept stage and doesn’t have any gameplay or footage to display just yet. We're actively building out the vision and would love your help bringing it to life. If you're interested in contributing, please visit our Careers page and reach out!
