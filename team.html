Meet the Team

Terry Teng

Founder & CEO

Terry started his career in the tech industry, but when the pandemic hit, he made the leap into game development to pursue a more passion-driven path. With years of experience managing digital products, he transitioned seamlessly into gaming and most recently served as Head of Production. Terry blends structure and storytelling to lead teams that build emotionally resonant, gameplay-first experiences.

Adam

Art Director

Adam is a talented artist with a passion for creating immersive worlds. He has a background in traditional art and has transitioned into digital art for games. Adam draws inspiration from nature and loves to incorporate organic elements into his designs.
